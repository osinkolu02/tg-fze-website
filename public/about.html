About Us

Who we are

A Nigerian export sourcing company that exists to make it easy for American and European buyers to get products out of Nigeria reliably.

Tangential Group FZE is a NEPZA-registered free zone enterprise based in Alaro City, Ibeju Lekki, Lagos. We help international buyers — manufacturers, importers, and distributors — source selected agricultural commodities, cocoa-butter ingredients, and basic apparel and textiles from Nigeria.

Why Nigeria

Nigeria is one of the world's largest producers of cashew nuts, sesame seeds, ginger, hibiscus, and cocoa. It's also AGOA-eligible for duty-free textile and apparel exports to the United States. But sourcing from Nigeria without a trusted local partner is difficult — unreliable suppliers, inconsistent quality, and opaque logistics. That's the problem we solve.

Free Zone Advantage

Operating from a NEPZA-registered free zone gives us streamlined customs procedures, simplified export documentation, and a regulatory environment designed for international trade. For you, that means faster clearance, fewer bureaucratic delays, and a more predictable export process.

How We Operate

We're selective about what we source and who we work with. Every engagement follows a documented process — requirement review, supplier vetting, sampling, quality inspection, documentation, and shipment coordination. We don't forward unverified products or make commitments we can't keep.

Our Broader Purpose

We believe export creates sustainable economic value and meaningful employment in Nigeria. By building reliable trade channels between Nigerian producers and global buyers, we help unlock capacity, create market access, and support productive economic activity in the communities where our suppliers operate.
