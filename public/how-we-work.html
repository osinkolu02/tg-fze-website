Our Process

How we get your product from Nigeria to your port

Every engagement follows the same structured process. No shortcuts, no improvisation. You'll know exactly what's happening at every stage.

1

Inquiry & Requirement Review

You tell us what you need — product, specifications, volume, quality expectations, delivery terms, and timeline. We review feasibility and respond within 48 hours. If we can't do it, we'll say so upfront.

2

Supplier Identification & Vetting

We match your requirements to suppliers in our vetted network. Each supplier is assessed for production capacity, quality history, and compliance readiness. We don't forward unverified sources.

3

Sampling & Specification Alignment

Product samples are sent for your review and testing. We work with you to lock in exact specifications, packaging, and labelling before production begins. No surprises downstream.

4

Quality Inspection & Documentation

Pre-shipment quality checks are conducted on every order. All documentation is prepared — phytosanitary certificates, certificates of origin, lab reports, fumigation records, bills of lading. Third-party inspection (SGS or equivalent) arranged on request.

5

Export Logistics & Delivery

We coordinate inland transport, container loading, customs clearance, and ocean freight. You receive tracking information and full shipping documentation. We stay available through to delivery at your destination port.
