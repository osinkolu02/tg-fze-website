Services

What we handle

Everything between your shipment leaving origin and your product reaching the Nigerian buyer. Nothing more, nothing less.

Cargo Receipt

Your shipment arrives at Lekki Deep Sea Port and moves through the Customs-approved Green Channel into our free-zone facility. Goods enter the Free Zone without customs duty for approved activities. We handle receiving, inspection against your packing list, and confirmation of goods received. You get a receipt report within 24 hours of arrival.

Storage

Secure, dry, monitored storage in a NEPZA-registered free-zone facility. Goods in the zone can be stored, packed, marked, re-marked, sold, and handled. Suitable for beauty products, personal care, packaged goods, packaging materials, and specialty ingredients. Ambient temperature, controlled environment — not cold chain.

Relabelling & Kitting

NAFDAC-compliant relabelling for products entering the Nigerian market. Kitting and bundling for promotional packs or distributor-specific configurations. We work to your specifications — you approve the label format before we start.

Inventory Reporting

Weekly stock reports showing what's in, what's been released, and what's remaining. No guessing, no chasing. You always know what you have on ground in Nigeria.

Controlled Release

You decide when stock moves. When goods are released from the Free Zone into Nigeria's customs territory, they become subject to the normal customs process and applicable duties. We help coordinate the required documentation — transaction request orders, invoices, and related forms. Release in batches as sales firm up, or on a schedule you set.

Dispatch Coordination

When you release stock, we coordinate dispatch to your distributor, retailer, or end customer within Lagos and beyond. We handle the last-mile logistics so you don't have to.

What we don't do

Honest boundaries

We don't handle cold chain, pharmaceuticals, hazardous chemicals, or bulk commodity storage. We don't do customs brokerage as a standalone service. We don't pretend to be a 3PL for all industries. We do inbound logistics, storage, and controlled release for the specific categories where our facility and process are the right fit. The Free Zone process is designed to be simpler and more supported, but it still involves documentation — we help coordinate that, not eliminate it.
