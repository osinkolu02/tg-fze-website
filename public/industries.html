How clients use us

Common ways companies work with us

Nigeria market entry

Receive imported stock into Lagos, hold it in a Free Zone facility, and release it into Nigeria when demand is ready. No need to build full local infrastructure before you have proven sales.

Staged inventory and controlled release

Avoid clearing full volumes at once. Hold stock close to market and release smaller quantities as orders come in. Pay duties only when goods move into Nigeria's customs territory.

Lagos as a regional distribution hub

For select clients, Lagos serves as an inventory base for Nigeria and nearby West African markets — with coordinated storage, visibility, release, and redistribution from one location.

Compliance and market-readiness support

Where required, we support relabelling and related preparation before market entry, helping ensure your products meet local requirements before release.

Other product categories

We review select adjacent categories on a case-by-case basis. If your product fits our storage environment and operating model, we're open to discussing it. Get in touch and we'll tell you honestly whether we're the right fit.
