Product Categories

What we source

Six focused categories. Not everything from Nigeria — just the products where we have verified suppliers and quality control.

Raw Cashew Nuts

W240, W320, W450 grades. Sourced from Oyo, Kwara, Kogi. For kernel processors and snack manufacturers.

Dried Split Ginger

High oleoresin content. Sourced from Kaduna, Nasarawa. For spice processors and food manufacturers.

Sesame Seeds

Hulled and natural, 99.95% purity. Sourced from Jigawa, Taraba. For oil extraction and food processing.

Dried Hibiscus Flower

Dark red Roselle. Sourced from Kano, Jigawa. For beverage production, tea blends, natural colourants.

Cocoa Butter Ingredients

Natural and deodorised grades. For chocolate manufacturers, confectionery, and cosmetics/personal-care formulators.

Basic Apparel & Textiles

T-shirts, scrubs, workwear, bed linen, towels. AGOA-eligible for duty-free U.S. access. For wholesalers and institutional buyers.
