How it works

A simple way to launch or grow in Lagos

1

Import into the Free Zone

Your goods arrive into the Free Zone for approved business use without customs duty at entry.

2

Store and prepare your stock

We receive, store, relabel, and organise your inventory inside the zone. Goods in the zone can be stored, packed, marked, re-marked, sold, and handled.

3

Release only what you need

When you're ready to sell into Nigeria, goods move from the Free Zone into the customs territory under the normal customs process. You control the timing.
